About Me

My name is Sina M. Baharlou and I am pursuing a Ph.D. in the Electrical and Computer Engineering at Boston University, working under the supervision of Prof. Abdoulaye Ndao. The focus of my research is on utilizing machine learning in photonics applications. Previously, I was a visiting researcher at the Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich where I collaborated on several research projects on Scene Graph Classification and Visual Relation Detection with Dr. Sahand Sharifzadeh and Prof. Volker Tresp.
